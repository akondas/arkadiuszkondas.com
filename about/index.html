About

Hi, my name is Arkadiusz Kondas and I am a Data Scientist and Software Architect from Poland. My main skills revolve around PHP and Machine Learning.

I am a open-source evangelist, an passionate programmer, a pragmatist and a good teacher.

I have been professionally involved in programming since 2008.

I led and created teams developing advanced information systems. I created, planned and developed high and low level architecture.

I change and develop enterprises using Event Storming.

I am currently working for Buddy, creating the best tools supporting the daily work of developers (CI/CD server).

In my free time I run in mountain ultramarathons.

I want to create software that allows you to change the world.

Those who are crazy enough to think they can change the world usually do.
